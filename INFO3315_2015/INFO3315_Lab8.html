Week 8: Enhance prototype, conduct Heuristic Evaluation to inform next prototype refinement

Homework

  1. Complete the practice exam, ready to hand it in in the lab.
  2. Complete any technical tasks from previous weeks as we will be moving ahead with new technical work next week.

Lab overview

  1. (5 minutes) Make sure you have written you SID on every page of your practice exam paper.
    Then hand it in to your tutor.
  2. (20 minutes) Based on the feedback from Assignment 1, refine the set of tasks you will ask a user to do when testing your system for Assignment 2.
    Take care to write them exactly as a user would read them if they were expected to do them.
    Put them on your Bitbucket site.
    Check the tasks you select with your tutor.
    3. (20 minutes) Prepare for the Heuristic Evaluation activity below.
    Select a set of at least 5 concrete tasks to use for the HE.
    Take time to polish one Balsamiq wireframe prototype created last week till it is ready for this HE evaluation.
  3. (55 minutes) Do inter-group Heuristic Evaluation of a Balsamiq wireframe prototype created last week.
    To dot his, split your group into 2 sub-groups.
    Members of your one sub-group act as the developers.
    One does the walkthroughs. Others in the subgroup record problems found, on Bitbucket.
    A subgroup from another group in the class will play the role of the evaluators.
    Reserve the last 10 minutes for the evaluators to rate the severity.
  4. (10 minutes) Record key outcomes of the Heuristic Evaluation on Bitbucket.
    Note changes you consider should be made to the design.

  Checkpoints
At the end of this lab session you should have completed:
  • Tasks recorded on Bitbucket
  • Results of the Heuristic Evaluation on Bitbucket
  • Group reflection on outcome of Heuristic Evaluation on Bitbucket.

Show each of these to your tutor on BitBucket and for feedback.
