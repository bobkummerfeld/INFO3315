Assumed Knowledge
The course does present and discuss technical issues of computer security. However, in order to successfully complete the course you only need to be a competent user of computer systems. It is not necessary to have a knowledge of programming.

However, you must have a willingness to follow explanations of technical subjects at a high level.

Lectures

The slide handout will be available on this web site a few days before the lecture.

The lectures will be recorded and recordings made available on your University e-learning web page.

Please make an effort to attend lectures and take your own notes. Lectures are an opportunity to ask questions and get more information about the topics being presented. Because the lectures are recorded and available online, many students decide to skip the live lecture and listen to the recording later. Unfortunately, it is very easy to forget to listen and fall behind. It is very hard to listen to 26 hours of lectures just before the final exam!
